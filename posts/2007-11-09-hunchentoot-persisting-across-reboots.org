In my earlier article on running Hunchentoot behind Apache, I mentioned
that it would not be very difficult to have Common Lisp persist your
runtime state across a system reboot. Well, after a bit of work, I now
have that support available. I've
[[http://www.newartisans.com/2007/11/running-common-lisp-behind-apache.html][revised
the article]] to reflect these changes, so please read there for more
information!
